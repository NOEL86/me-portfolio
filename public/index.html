About

I have spent 8 years working in the Criminal Justice System. I started with theft investigations working with local law enforcement to solve fraud and forgery cases as well as internal and external store thefts in Asset Protection. I then worked for the Nevada Department of Corrections as an Officer in a Women's minimum security camp where I was awarded employee of the year. I moved up to Correctional Caseworker where I assisted in managing 336 inmates in my unit at High Desert State Prison. I am now working as a Probation Case Manager for Salt Lake County. I manage a caseload of clients with misdemeanor offenses and assist them to complete their probation successfully and make positive life changes. I completed a full stack web developement program with the University of Utah. I am now Searching for opportunities in Web Development where I can utilize my skills in Javascript, NodeJS, ReactJS, HTML5, and CSS3.
